Our Testing Methodology

Choose the approach that fits your security posture and compliance requirements

Black-Box Testing

Simulates external attacker with zero knowledge of your systems. Tests how well your defenses hold against unknown threats trying to breach from the outside.

‚ö™

Gray-Box Testing

Balanced approach with limited system knowledge (like credentials for different user roles). Maximizes coverage while simulating realistic threat scenarios. Most popular for compliance audits.

White-Box Testing

Full access including source code, architecture diagrams, and credentials. Provides deepest security analysis and catches vulnerabilities automated tools miss.
